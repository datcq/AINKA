sớm sau đột quỵ7

Nghiên cứu đa trung tâm, ngẫu nhiên, song song, mù đôi, n=927 trong 12 tuần Phân nhóm 452 bệnh nhân được điều trị sớm với Piracetam (trong vòng 7 h sau khi khởi phát đột quỵ) Điều trị: liều đầu 12g IV Piracetam hoặc Placebo, 12 g Piracetam/ngày x 8 tuần và 4,8 g Piracetam/ngày x 4 tuần

  • The mean Orgogozo scale score after 4 weeks was 5.5 points greater in the piracetam (60.4±32.0) than in the placebo group (54.9±32.4), a difference approaching significance (P=.07)
  • The mean Barthel Index score at 12 weeks was significantly higher in the active group (58.6±42.8) compared with placebo (49.4±43.2) (P=0.02).

Piracetam is useful in early period after stroke7

Randomized, multicenter, double-blind, parallel, n=927 patients, in 12 weeks. Subgroup of 452 patients who were treated early with piracetam (within 7 hours after stroke). 12g/daily of Piracetam IV/placebo in 8 weeks and 4,8 g piracetam IV/day in 4 weeks
